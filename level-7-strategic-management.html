Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership

120 credits | 12-18 months | Equivalent to Master's/MBA Taught Stage | 100% Online

Overview

The OTHM Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership is a postgraduate-level qualification designed for experienced professionals and managers who want to develop strategic leadership capabilities. Equivalent to the taught stage of a UK Master's degree or MBA, this programme equips you with the knowledge and skills to lead at the highest level.

What You Will Learn

Core Units

Strategic Management, Strategic Leadership, Strategic Human Resource Management, Strategic Financial Management, Advanced Business Research Methods.

Specialist Units

Strategic Marketing, Strategic Change Management, Developing Organisational Strategy and Performance, Strategic Quality and Systems Management.

Key Facts

Qualification: OTHM Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership
Credits: 120
Duration: 12-18 months (self-paced)
Mode: 100% Online via Moodle
Assessment: Written assignments - no examinations
Entry Requirements: Bachelor's degree or Level 6 qualification, or significant management experience (5+ years); age 21+; English proficiency (IELTS 6.0 equivalent)

Progression Routes

  • Top up to a full MBA or Master's - Complete a dissertation at a UK university to earn a full MBA or MA/MSc (typically 6-12 months).
  • Senior Leadership Roles - Use the qualification to pursue or advance in director-level, C-suite, or senior management positions.
  • Professional Recognition - Demonstrate strategic capability to employers, clients, and professional bodies.
