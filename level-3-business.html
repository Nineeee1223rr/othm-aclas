Level 3 Foundation Diploma in Business

60 credits | 6-9 months | A-Level / High School Equivalent | 100% Online

Overview

The OTHM Level 3 Foundation Diploma in Business is an introductory qualification designed for school leavers, career changers, and anyone new to business study. It provides a broad understanding of business fundamentals and prepares you for progression to Level 4 study or entry-level employment.

What You Will Learn

Units

  • Introduction to Business
  • Marketing Principles
  • Business Finance
  • Enterprise and Entrepreneurship
  • Communication for Business
  • Understanding the Business Environment

Key Facts

Qualification: Level 3 Foundation Diploma in Business
Credits: 60 credits
Duration: 6-9 months
Mode: 100% Online via Moodle
Assessment: Written assignments - no examinations
Awarding Body: OTHM Qualifications (Ofqual regulated)
Entry Requirements: See individual course page for details

Progression Routes

  • Progress to Level 4 - Continue to an OTHM Level 4 Diploma in Business Management.
  • Enter employment - Use your qualification for entry-level roles in administration, customer service, sales, or retail.
  • Foundation for university - Use as a pathway to a university foundation programme.
