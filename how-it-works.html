How It Works

From enrolment to certification, every step of your journey is clear, supported, and flexible.

Your Journey in Four Steps

Move from enquiry to certification through a clear online study path, with support at every stage.

1. Enrol

Browse our courses and choose your qualification. Complete the online enrolment form and choose your payment option. Receive your Moodle login within 24 hours.

2. Learn

Access all course materials on Moodle - readings, videos, case studies, quizzes. Study at your own pace. Participate in forums and optional live webinars. Contact your Tutor anytime.

3. Assess

Submit assignments through Moodle. Receive detailed written feedback within 15 working days. Your work is internally quality-assured before final confirmation.

4. Certify

Your work undergoes OTHM External Quality Assurance. On confirmation, receive your digital certificate and transcript - free of charge. Use your qualification to progress to university or advance your career.

How Assessment Works

Fair, transparent, and constructive - our assessment process is designed to help you succeed.

What Happens When You Submit

1. You upload your assignment through Moodle. 2. Your work is checked for plagiarism. 3. Your Assessor marks it against published criteria. 4. You receive written feedback within 15 working days. 5. If you pass, you move on. If resubmission is needed, you get clear guidance and another opportunity - at no extra charge.

Quality Checks

Every assessment decision is verified by our Lead IQA and subject to external scrutiny by an OTHM-appointed External Quality Assurer. The grade on your certificate has been checked and confirmed at multiple levels.
