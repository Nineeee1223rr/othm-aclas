Level 4 & 5 Diploma in Health and Social Care Management

240 credits (120 per level) | 12-18 months | Equivalent to Years 1 & 2 of a UK Bachelor's Degree | 100% Online

Overview

The OTHM Level 4 and Level 5 Diploma in Health and Social Care Management provides a comprehensive foundation for a career in health, social care, and community settings. Equivalent to the first two years of a UK bachelor's degree, this combined programme equips you with the knowledge and skills to manage effectively in health and care environments.

What You Will Learn

Units

  • Health and Social Care in Context
  • Communication Skills in Health and Social Care
  • Safeguarding in Health and Social Care
  • Managing Quality in Health and Social Care
  • Leadership and Management for Health and Social Care
  • Professional Development in Health and Social Care

Additional Units

  • Managing Finance in Health and Social Care
  • Partnership Working in Health and Social Care
  • Research Methods for Health and Social Care
  • Managing Change in Health and Social Care
  • Health Promotion and Public Health
  • Contemporary Issues in Health and Social Care

Key Facts

Qualification: Level 4 & 5 Diploma in Health and Social Care Management
Credits: 240 credits (120 per level)
Duration: 12-18 months
Mode: 100% Online via Moodle
Assessment: Written assignments - no examinations
Awarding Body: OTHM Qualifications (Ofqual regulated)
Entry Requirements: See individual course page for details

Progression Routes

  • Top up to a full Bachelor's degree - Final-year entry to a BA (Hons) Health and Social Care at a UK university.
  • Enter management roles - Pursue positions in care homes, hospitals, community health organisations, or social services.
  • Continue to postgraduate study - Progress to Level 6 or Level 7 qualifications for further specialisation.
