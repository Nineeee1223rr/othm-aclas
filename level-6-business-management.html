Level 6 Diploma in Business Management

120 credits | 9-12 months | Equivalent to the Final Year of a UK Bachelor's Degree | 100% Online

Overview

The OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Business Management is a graduate-level qualification, equivalent to the final year of a UK bachelor's degree. It is designed for learners who already hold a Level 5 qualification (or equivalent) and want to complete a full degree-level diploma or prepare for postgraduate study.

What You Will Learn

Units

  • Strategic Marketing
  • Corporate Finance and Financial Strategy
  • International Business Environment
  • Organisational Behaviour
  • Strategic Human Resource Management
  • Dissertation / Research Project

Key Facts

Qualification: Level 6 Diploma in Business Management
Credits: 120 credits
Duration: 9-12 months
Mode: 100% Online via Moodle
Assessment: Written assignments - no examinations
Awarding Body: OTHM Qualifications (Ofqual regulated)
Entry Requirements: See individual course page for details

Progression Routes

  • Top up to a full BA/BSc - Use this diploma for final-year entry to a bachelor's degree at a UK university.
  • Progress to postgraduate study - Qualify for entry to OTHM Level 7 or a Master's programme.
  • Career advancement - Demonstrate graduate-level knowledge to current or prospective employers.
