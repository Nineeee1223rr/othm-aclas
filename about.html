About ACLAS Global International Education Group

We are on a mission to make high-quality, internationally recognised UK qualifications accessible to learners everywhere - regardless of location, schedule, or background.

Our Story

ACLAS Global International Education Group was founded with a simple belief: that a high-quality, internationally recognised qualification should not require relocating to another country or putting your life on hold.

ACLAS Global International Education Group partners with OTHM Qualifications - a UK-regulated awarding organisation recognised by Ofqual - to deliver diploma programmes from Level 3 (foundation) to Level 7 (postgraduate).

We serve learners from the United States and around the world. All our programmes are delivered 100% online through our Moodle learning platform, supported by qualified Tutors and Assessors. Our learners study at their own pace, from home, while balancing work, family, and other commitments.

What makes us different? We combine the academic rigour of UK-accredited qualifications with the flexibility and accessibility of modern online learning - and the personal, one-to-one support that every learner deserves.

Staff English Language Competence

All OTHM qualifications are delivered, assessed, and quality-assured in English. ACLAS Global ensures that all staff involved in the delivery, assessment, or internal quality assurance of OTHM qualifications possess the necessary English language skills to:

  • Understand and accurately apply OTHM assessment criteria
  • Mark assessments and differentiate learner performance
  • Provide clear, constructive written feedback to learners
  • Understand and act on feedback from OTHM and the External Quality Assurer
  • Communicate effectively with OTHM in all correspondence, meetings, and quality assurance activities

English language competence is assessed during recruitment and monitored through ongoing quality assurance activities. The Centre does not rely on interpreters or translation services for any communication with OTHM or for the assessment of learner work.

Quality Assurance

As an OTHM candidate centre, ACLAS Global International Education Group is subject to ongoing quality assurance:

Internal Quality Assurance (IQA) - Every assessment decision is sampled by our Lead IQA to ensure consistency and fairness.
External Quality Assurance (EQA) - An OTHM-appointed EQA periodically reviews our assessment processes and samples learner work.
Annual Self-Assessment - We conduct a thorough self-review against OTHM's Centre Approval Criteria each year.
Standardisation - Our Assessors meet regularly to cross-mark work and ensure consistent grading.
