Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Who We Are

ACLAS Global International Education Group ("we", "us", "our") operates the website aclas.global. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data fairly, lawfully, and transparently.

2. What Data We Collect

When you visit our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (email, phone, postal address) when you complete our enquiry form
  • Information about your use of our website (pages visited, time spent) through cookies and analytics
  • Information you provide when you communicate with us by any means

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to: respond to your enquiries about our programmes; provide you with information you have requested; improve our website and services; and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for marketing unless you have explicitly consented.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Enquiry data is retained for up to 2 years.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations); and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aclas.global.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., OTHM, Moodle). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

8. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@aclas.global.
