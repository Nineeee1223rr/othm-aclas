What Learners Can Expect

A transparent guide to the learner experience at ACLAS Global International Education Group.

Your Learning Environment

At ACLAS Global, every learner studies through Moodle — the world's most widely used open-source learning platform, trusted by universities and organisations globally. You will have 24/7 access to your course materials, assignments, grades, and Tutor communication from any device.

Personal Tutor Support

Every learner is assigned a named Tutor/Assessor who:

  • Guides your learning journey from enrolment to certification
  • Marks your assignments against published criteria
  • Provides detailed written feedback within 15 working days
  • Is available for one-to-one discussion via Moodle messaging, email, or scheduled video calls

Assessment and Quality Assurance

Your work goes through multiple layers of quality checking:

  • Assessor marking — Your named Assessor marks your work against OTHM's published assessment criteria
  • Internal Quality Assurance (IQA) — The Lead IQA samples assessment decisions to ensure consistency and fairness
  • External Quality Assurance (EQA) — An OTHM-appointed External Quality Assurer periodically reviews the Centre's assessment processes

Academic Integrity

All written submissions are checked through plagiarism-detection software integrated with Moodle. Learners receive guidance on academic integrity, referencing, and proper use of AI tools during induction. Suspected malpractice is handled under our published Malpractice and Plagiarism Policy.

Flexibility and Support

  • Study at your own pace — no fixed class times
  • Extensions and adjusted deadlines available when life happens
  • Reasonable adjustments for learners with additional needs
  • Monthly instalment plans at no extra cost

Recognition

OTHM qualifications are regulated by Ofqual and listed on the UK Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF). Your certificate is identical to that issued to learners at any OTHM-approved centre worldwide.

As a candidate centre preparing for OTHM approval, we are building our learner community. We look forward to sharing authentic learner experiences once our first cohort has completed their programmes.
