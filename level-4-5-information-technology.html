Level 4 & 5 Diploma in Information Technology

240 credits (120 per level) | 12-18 months | Equivalent to Years 1 & 2 of a UK Bachelor's Degree | 100% Online

Overview

The OTHM Level 4 and Level 5 Diploma in Information Technology provides a comprehensive foundation in computing, programming, and IT management. Equivalent to the first two years of a UK bachelor's degree, this programme prepares you for a career in software development, cybersecurity, IT management, or data analytics.

What You Will Learn

Units

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Computer Systems and Networks
  • Web Development
  • Database Systems
  • Information Systems Analysis and Design
  • IT Security Management

Additional Units

  • Software Engineering
  • Cybersecurity
  • Cloud Computing
  • Project Management
  • Data Analytics
  • E-Commerce and Digital Business

Key Facts

Qualification: Level 4 & 5 Diploma in Information Technology
Credits: 240 credits (120 per level)
Duration: 12-18 months
Mode: 100% Online via Moodle
Assessment: Written assignments - no examinations
Awarding Body: OTHM Qualifications (Ofqual regulated)
Entry Requirements: See individual course page for details

Progression Routes

  • Top up to a full BSc degree - Final-year entry to a BSc (Hons) in Computing or IT at a UK university.
  • Enter the tech industry - Pursue roles in software development, IT support, network administration, or cybersecurity.
  • Progress to Level 6 or 7 - Continue to graduate or postgraduate diplomas in IT, data science, or cybersecurity.
