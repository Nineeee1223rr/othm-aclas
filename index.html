Quick Answers

What does ACLAS Global offer?

ACLAS Global is an OTHM candidate centre preparing to deliver online diploma qualifications from Level 3 to Level 7. Programmes are designed for flexible study with tutor support, assignment-based assessment, and university progression routes.

How are OTHM qualifications studied?

ACLAS Global programmes use online learning through Moodle. Learners study course materials at their own pace, submit coursework assignments, receive written feedback from a Tutor/Assessor, and complete quality-assured units without fixed class times.

Where can OTHM diplomas lead?

OTHM diplomas sit on the UK Regulated Qualifications Framework. Depending on the level completed, learners may use an OTHM qualification for advanced entry to bachelor's, top-up degree, master's, or MBA routes at receiving universities.

01

Ofqual-Regulated Qualifications

Every qualification sits on the UK Regulated Qualifications Framework - the same framework governing GCSEs, A-Levels, and university degrees. Your diploma carries identical academic recognition worldwide.

02

Designed for Working Lives

No fixed class times. No travel. All materials, assignments, and communication on our Moodle platform, accessible 24 hours a day. Study when it suits you.

03

Personal Tutor Throughout

From enrolment to certification, a qualified Tutor and Assessor guides your development, marks your work, and provides written feedback. You are never left to figure things out alone.

Programme Levels

OTHM qualifications span six levels of the UK Regulated Qualifications Framework.

Level 4 & 5

Undergraduate

First and second year of a bachelor's degree. Complete this 240-credit diploma and top up to a full BA or BSc at university - typically one final year. Our most popular pathway.

Level 3

Foundation

Pre-university entry. Equivalent to A-Levels. An introduction to academic study in business and related fields.

Level 6

Graduate

Final year of a bachelor's degree. For those who hold a Level 5 qualification and wish to complete a full degree-level diploma.

Level 7

Postgraduate

Master's and MBA level. For experienced professionals seeking strategic leadership roles or doctoral preparation. Top up to a full MBA or Master's degree at a UK university.

Subject Areas

Four principal disciplines, each with clear progression to university degrees and professional careers.

Business

Business Management

From Level 3 Foundation through to Level 7 Strategic Management and Leadership. Leading to careers in management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

Health & Care

Health & Social Care Management

Knowledge and skills for management roles in hospitals, care homes, community health organisations, and social services.

Technology

Information Technology

Foundations in programming, networking, cybersecurity, and data analytics - with progression to a BSc in Computing.

Education

Education & Training

Qualify as a teacher or trainer in the further education and skills sector. From introductory awards to the full Level 5 Diploma.
