Programme Levels

OTHM qualifications span six levels of the UK Regulated Qualifications Framework.

Level 4 & 5

Undergraduate

First and second year of a bachelor's degree. Complete this 240-credit diploma and top up to a full BA or BSc at university - typically one final year. Our most popular pathway.

Level 3

Foundation

Pre-university entry. Equivalent to A-Levels. An introduction to academic study in business and related fields.

Level 6

Graduate

Final year of a bachelor's degree. For those who hold a Level 5 qualification and wish to complete a full degree-level diploma.

Level 7

Postgraduate

Master's and MBA level. For experienced professionals seeking strategic leadership roles or doctoral preparation. Top up to a full MBA or Master's degree at a UK university.

Subject Areas

Four principal disciplines, each with clear progression to university degrees and professional careers.

Business

Business Management

From Level 3 Foundation through to Level 7 Strategic Management and Leadership. Leading to careers in management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

Health & Care

Health & Social Care Management

Knowledge and skills for management roles in hospitals, care homes, community health organisations, and social services.

Technology

Information Technology

Foundations in programming, networking, cybersecurity, and data analytics - with progression to a BSc in Computing.

Education

Education & Training

Qualify as a teacher or trainer in the further education and skills sector. From introductory awards to the full Level 5 Diploma.
